Gerrymandering: The Game

Jul 3, 2016

My sister’s boyfriend and I had this idea for a game about gerrymandering for a while. I have tried my best to explain it through slides and an HTML5 video demo.

I think this would be a great tool for the classroom. A quick game that would teach people how gerrymandering works. I have suspended work on this project, until I can find more teachers interested in using this in their classrooms. Feel free to reach out at my contact info in the footer.
