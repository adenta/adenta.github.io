About

Hi! I am Andrew Denta. I am a Full Stack Web Engineer, currently studying at Indiana University: Bloomington. I spend a lot of my time working with Serve IT, a Bloomington organization that makes websites & webapps for non profits. I am currently in charge of training the new developers who are joining Serve IT. I also am apart of the IU Student Ambassadors, where we help out the schools recruiting efforts.

In my free time, I like to work on projects I find interesting. Recently we tried to start a business based around using advertising to fund charity: Add.Ninja. Feel free to check out some of my past projects and tutorials.

For fun, I am a big fan of making balloon animals! I also play the Euphonium, but have not been keeping up with practicing it lately.
